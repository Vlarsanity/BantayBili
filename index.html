A Live Shopping Platform for Filipinos

BantayBili is an online shopping platform that uses live video selling to connect buyers directly with Filipino sellers.

Every product you see is made in the Philippines. Imported goods are not allowed. Buyers can watch products being presented in real time, ask questions, and see the actual quality before purchasing.

Who We Serve

Local Sellers

BantayBili supports Filipino makers such as weavers, Marikina shoemakers, farmers, and artists who struggle to compete with cheap imported goods.

Local Buyers

Our buyers are Filipinos who want to support the local economy but are afraid of online scams and misleading product photos.

A Trusted Local Community

While other platforms act like global warehouses, BantayBili functions as a local Filipino community.

  • Live selling removes guesswork and scams
  • Sellers show real products in real time
  • Workshops and farms are shown to verify authenticity
Seller showing workshop or farm on live video

From Local to Global

BantayBili aims to bring Filipino craftsmanship to the world. Instead of buying from other countries, we want other countries to buy Tatak Pinoy products from us.

Through live streams, international buyers can see, trust, and purchase Filipino-made products directly from local sellers.

Filipino product with global audience

Making Local Products Competitive

Shipping between Philippine islands is expensive. Funding support will be used to provide a shipping subsidy for local sellers.

Lower shipping costs allow Filipino products to compete fairly with imported items while supporting small businesses nationwide.

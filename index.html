What We Do

A Live Shopping Platform for Filipinos

BantayBili is an online shopping platform that uses live video selling to connect buyers directly with Filipino sellers.

Every product you see is made in the Philippines. Imported goods are not allowed. Buyers can watch products being presented in real time, ask questions, and see the actual quality before purchasing.

Our Vision

From Local to Global

BantayBili aims to bring Filipino craftsmanship to the world. Instead of buying from other countries, we want other countries to buy Tatak Pinoy products from us.

Through live streams, international buyers can see, trust, and purchase Filipino-made products directly from local sellers.

Our Goal: Make "Made in Philippines" a global mark of quality and authenticity.

Investment Use

Making Local Products Competitive

Shipping between Philippine islands is expensive, often making local products more costly than imported goods.

Funding support will be used to provide a shipping subsidy for local sellers, making Filipino-made products competitive on price while maintaining quality and supporting small businesses nationwide.
